User Policy and Privacy Information

At Pack Generator, we prioritize your privacy and security. Here's how we handle your data:

Media Uploads

  • No Storage: Any media files you upload are not stored on our servers or databases. They are processed temporarily and discarded afterward.
  • Client-Side Processing: Media uploads are handled locally on your device or browser, ensuring that your files are not transmitted to our servers.

Input Information

  • Temporary Cache: Any information you input on our website is stored temporarily in your browser's cache. This data is not transmitted to or stored on our servers.
  • No Data Retention: We do not retain any information you input, including personal data, after you close your browser or end your session.

Security Measures

  • Secure Connections: Our website uses secure connections (HTTPS) to protect your data from unauthorized access.
  • No Data Sharing: We do not share any user data with third parties.

Your Rights

  • Data Control: You have full control over your data, and we provide you with the tools to manage your information.
  • Right to Erase: If you wish to erase any temporary data stored in your browser's cache, you can do so by clearing your browser's cache.

Changes to this Policy

  • Updates: We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws.
  • Notice: We'll notify you of any significant changes to this policy.

By using our website, you acknowledge that you've read and understood our user policy and privacy information. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at sillencedplayz@gmail.com.
